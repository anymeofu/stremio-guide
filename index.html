What is Stremio?

Stremio is a free, open-source media center app. It gathers information about movies and TV shows from sources like IMDb and organizes them into a user-friendly catalog. Its real power comes from a vast library of community-built addons that provide streaming links.

Important Notes About Using Stremio and Real-Debrid

Note:This is a streaming-only service, not a long-term video storage solution. For the best experience, we recommend a paid subscription.

This service is optimized for streaming, not for archiving large video libraries.

If you experience buffering, please check your internet connection and device performance. In most cases, these issues are related to local factors.
